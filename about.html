Dr. Bhadra Bhandari is a respected Nepali leader whose life has been dedicated to public service, women's empowerment, and social reform. As the Chairperson of Women Wisdom Nepal (Nari Drishti Nepal), she has been at the forefront of supporting widowed and marginalized women through legal aid, emotional support, and economic independence programs. Her mission is rooted in dignity, equality, and opportunity for all.

In 2081 B.S., she was elected Treasurer of the Nepali Congress in Bagmati Province, where she plays a key role in provincial political decision-making and advocates for gender-inclusive governance. Dr. Bhandari has also served in humanitarian work for over 24 years as a committed member of Lions Clubs Nepal, organizing numerous welfare programs across the country.

Her contribution to the media sector as a former Director of Capital FM reflects her multifaceted leadership in shaping both public opinion and social progress. Known for her calm demeanor and strong sense of purpose, Dr. Bhandari continues to inspire a new generation of Nepali women leaders. Her journey is one of resilience, dedication, and impact—marking her as a significant voice in Nepal’s ongoing development story.

Role at Nepali Congress

In 2081 B.S., Dr. Bhadra Bhandari was elected as the Treasurer of the Nepali Congress Bagmati Province, a significant milestone that reflects the party’s confidence in her leadership and unwavering commitment to public service. Her appointment to this key role has solidified her standing as a respected political figure, especially in a time where transparency, accountability, and inclusivity are vital for institutional growth.

As Treasurer, Dr. Bhandari has taken the lead in promoting financial transparency and strengthening internal organizational structures. She has worked tirelessly to manage funds responsibly, ensure proper resource allocation, and improve financial systems within the party. Beyond numbers, her efforts have helped support grassroots mobilization and development initiatives across diverse communities within the province.

Dr. Bhandari also uses her platform to advocate for the inclusion of women and marginalized groups in political processes. She remains a vocal proponent of social justice, equity, and democratic reform, helping shape policies that represent a broader spectrum of voices. Her active participation in provincial governance highlights her dedication to fostering a more accountable and people-centered Nepali Congress.

Efforts and Effect in Lions Club Nepal

Dr. Bhadra Bhandari has been a dedicated and passionate member of Lions Club Nepal for over 24 years. Throughout her tenure, she has demonstrated unwavering commitment to supporting various humanitarian causes across the country. From organizing and leading health camps that provide medical care to underserved communities to actively participating in disaster relief programs during times of national crises, Dr. Bhandari has played a key role in ensuring that essential services reach those in need. As a passionate advocate for women’s empowerment, she has worked tirelessly to support marginalized communities, focusing particularly on empowering women and children through education, healthcare, and community development initiatives. By championing women’s rights and advocating for equal access to opportunities, Dr. Bhandari has inspired countless women to take on leadership roles within their communities and make a meaningful impact in various sectors, from politics to business.

Former Director at Capital FM

Listen to Capital FM

As the former Director of Capital FM, Dr. Bhadra Bhandari played a pivotal role in revolutionizing the media landscape in Nepal. She was instrumental in designing and executing programming strategies that resonated with a diverse audience, ensuring that the station remained a voice for the people. Under her leadership, Capital FM became known for its engaging content, cultural programming, and social impact campaigns.

Dr. Bhandari’s time at Capital FM also marked a period of community engagement. She introduced programs that addressed important societal issues, including women’s rights, education, and health. Her ability to blend entertainment with social advocacy made Capital FM a platform where listeners could connect, learn, and grow.
